Our Mission

To make India's rich cultural heritage accessible, engaging, and sustainable for everyone through immersive technology.

The Story of TravelVerse

TravelVerse was born from a passion for history and a belief in the power of technology to tell stories. For the Smart India Hackathon 2025, our team, TravelVerse, took on the challenge of cultural heritage preservation[cite: 9, 3, 4]. We aim to bridge the gap between physical monuments and digital natives, creating a platform that not only preserves our heritage but also empowers the communities that uphold these traditions.

Our Core Pillars of Impact

Preservation

Creating digital archives to ensure timeless access and protect monuments from physical degradation[cite: 190].

Education

Making learning about history interactive and fun through gamification and affordable virtual field trips[cite: 187].

Economy

Supporting local artisan economies through an integrated marketplace, creating new revenue streams[cite: 194].
